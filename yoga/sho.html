For More Cool, Calm, and Collectedness

1. Take a seat in an upright position.

2. Move your left arm until (almost) fully stretched infront of the right side of your chest and right shoulder.

3. Grip onto your left upper arm with your right hand and apply light pressure. Hold this position for 15 seconds.

4.-6. Change sides and repeat the first three steps.

And now you can continue your day with less tension in this region!
