For More Cool, Calm, and Collectedness

1

1. Sit upright on a chair.

2. Hold your hands as if you were reaching out for yourself, and then stretch your arms out directly in front of you.

3. Take a deep breath in and then lift your arms up vertically. Hold this position for two to three breaths.

4. After a few breaths, slowly bring your arms down whilst exhaling.

Relax for a moment, and then repeat this exercise two more times. And then, we hope you have a lovely day!
