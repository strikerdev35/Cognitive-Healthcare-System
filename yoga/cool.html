For More Cool, Calm, and Collectedness

1

1. Take a deep and slow breath in, inhaling through your nose, whilst counting to 4 in your head.

2. Hold your breath there for 7 seconds.

3. Then slowly breath out through your mouth. While doing this, count to 8 in your head.

Repeat from step 1 three more times and take this feeling of calm with you into your day.
